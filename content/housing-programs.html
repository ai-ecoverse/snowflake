Get Help · Housing Programs
The Road Home believes housing is the solution to homelessness.
Our programs help families and individuals move into housing and focus on short-term solutions that lead to long-term stability. If you or someone you know is struggling to stay housed, please call Utah Community Action.

Our programs
Four paths into housing.
Each program matches a different household and circumstance. Call or get referred; a housing navigator will help you figure out which one fits.
#
01
Housing Navigation Services
Guests staying at our facilities have access to general housing support and specialized housing navigation. We help review housing history, understand what someone qualifies for, and find housing that fits their budget.
#
02
Rapid Rehousing
Short-term rental assistance paired with case management to help households move from shelter to stable housing — quickly. Most households exit the program within 6 months.
/palmer-court
03
Permanent Supportive Housing
Long-term subsidized housing with wrap-around support for adults and families with a history of chronic homelessness. Palmer Court (201 units) is our flagship.
#
04
SSVF — Veterans
Supportive Services for Veteran Families: rapid rehousing and case management for veteran households. Eligibility is based on veteran status and income.
What housing navigation covers
Housing navigators are specialists who know what housing exists in Salt Lake County, what it costs, and what paperwork it requires. They work alongside case managers — never instead of.
Review
Housing history review
We help guests gather and review their housing history to support applications and eligibility.
Search
Personalized housing search
We work with each household to understand what kind of housing they're looking for and what fits their budget.
Apply
Application support
We help complete applications, gather required documentation, and coordinate with landlords.
Landlord
Landlord coordination
We mediate move-in paperwork and maintain a point of contact if questions come up after move-in.
Move-in
Move-in support
Security deposit help, move-in kits, and first-month rent assistance through partner funding.
Stabilize
Tenancy support
First 6–12 months of tenancy: check-ins, budgeting help, and crisis intervention if needed.
For landlords
Partner with us.
We partner with landlords across Salt Lake County to place our guests in safe, habitable housing. Landlord partners receive a reliable point of contact, move-in-ready tenants, and direct rent-assistance payments during the transition period.
